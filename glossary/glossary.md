# Glossary

## Structural Capacity
The ability of a business to scale revenue and volume without a proportional increase in founder effort or operational chaos.

## Scale Stronger, Not Heavier
The core category claim. Growth that builds structural integrity rather than accumulating founder dependency and operational drag.

## The Bottleneck
The single structural constraint limiting a business's ability to scale. Usually the founder or a lack of decision rights.

## Decision Rights
Explicit assignment of who owns which call. Removes the founder as the default answer.

## SCALE Method
The proprietary operating system: Surface, Connect, Assign, Lead, Expand.
