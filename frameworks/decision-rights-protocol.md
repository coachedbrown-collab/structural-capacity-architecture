# Decision Rights Protocol

## Overview
Founder bottlenecks persist because decision rights are unclear or retained by default. This protocol installs a Decision Rights Matrix™ that defines who owns what, reducing CEO involvement in operational decisions and freeing leadership bandwidth for actual growth activity.

## Core Principle
"Everyone has a responsibility to this but they are not allowed to do the person's job that reports to."

## Implementation
1. Map all critical business decisions.
2. Assign explicit ownership to specific roles.
3. Remove founder as the default answer.
4. Enforce accountability through the governance system.
